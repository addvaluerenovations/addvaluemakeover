The Four-Week Programme

One team, one schedule, no idle days.

Each stage runs straight into the next — the day one trade finishes, the next starts. That's how a normal renovation gets squeezed into a month.

Week One
01

Repair & make-good

We fix everything that makes a buyer hesitate — before anything gets painted. Sound house first, pretty house second.

  • Patch & plaster
  • Leaks & rot
  • Sticky doors & windows
  • Surface prep
Week Two
02

Interior paint & refresh

Walls, ceilings, trim and doors sprayed and rolled in a fresh, neutral palette — plus kitchen & bathroom resurfacing where it lifts value.

  • Walls & ceilings
  • Trim & doors
  • Cabinet resurfacing
  • Re-seal wet areas
Week Three
03

Exterior paint

Weatherboards or render, joinery, eaves and the front door — the kerb appeal that decides whether buyers even walk inside.

  • Wash & prep
  • Weatherboards / render
  • Joinery & trim
  • Front door
Week Four
04

Final fit-off

Handles, fixtures, splashbacks, seals and finishing touches go on. Then we clean, snag and hand the house back.

  • Handles & fixtures
  • Splashbacks
  • Snag & touch-up
  • Full clean
